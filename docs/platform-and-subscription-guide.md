# AI Platform & Subscription Guide for Nonprofits

> **Stub.** Sanitized, client-neutral version in progress.

A decision guide for a nonprofit adopting AI, answering four questions in order:

1. **No productivity platform yet?** Choose Microsoft 365 for Nonprofits vs Google Workspace for Nonprofits (both free for eligible orgs).
2. **When to pay** for a standalone assistant beyond the free AI bundled with your grant.
3. **Which provider** — and the standalone-vs-in-suite trade-off.
4. **If you own a custom app maintained by AI** — which coding agent (start free), and where to host (Vercel + Neon regardless of office suite, for the preview-database safety rail).

The `np-ai-discovery` skill turns this guide into a short interview that wires the right connections for a client.
