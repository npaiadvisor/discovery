# The Nonprofit AI Adoption Journey

> **Stub.** Sanitized, client-neutral version in progress.

A four-stage map for how a nonprofit moves from *"we should probably do something with AI"* to *"we operate, maintain, and evolve our own AI assistant without outside help."*

1. **Public workshop** — staff see practical AI hands-on; warm leads self-select.
2. **Client workshop** — a tailored, hands-on session on the org's own tools and tasks; surfaces the highest-value opportunity.
3. **Build** — co-build one assistant against a specific objective, prototype → production.
4. **Sustain** — the org owns and evolves the assistant on its own infrastructure.

The first two stages reach many organizations at low cost; only the build stage needs deep one-client time.
